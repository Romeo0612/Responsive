Ming’s drive to simplify and improve people’s lives was born from her own journey toward self-actualization. And talk about meeting a need — according to the organization's website, she has connected 40,000 people from over 100 countries through the online professional community. And, the organizations events draw in heavy-hitting speakers like Fair Fight founder Stacey Abrams and TransTech founder Angelica Ross. Pittsford is definitely not just “talking the talk” when it comes to instigating change.
